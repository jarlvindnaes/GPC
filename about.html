About Product Connect

Product Connect is the infrastructure layer for Digital Product Passports — connecting raw materials, supply chains, and end-users in a single source of truth.

Our story

Born from making things

Product Connect grew out of Stykka — a Copenhagen-based design and manufacturing company that builds physical products for some of Europe's most demanding brands.

Working daily with BOMs, supplier networks, and material specifications, we saw the gap firsthand: manufacturers have deep knowledge of their products, but no infrastructure to share it — with regulators, customers, or even their own teams.

So we built Product Connect. Not as a compliance checkbox, but as a platform that turns product knowledge into a competitive advantage.

What we believe

Every decision we make is shaped by a set of principles about how product data should work.

01

Transparency creates trust

When customers can see where a product comes from, what it's made of, and how it impacts the planet — they don't just buy it. They believe in it. Transparency is the new brand equity.

02

Data should flow, not sit

Product data locked in spreadsheets and ERPs doesn't help anyone. We believe product intelligence should flow from BOM to passport to consumer to recycler — seamlessly.

03

Compliance is a starting line

Meeting ESPR requirements is table stakes. The real opportunity is using your product graph to reduce impact, find better suppliers, open new revenue channels, and build products that last longer.

The EU context

Built for the regulatory reality

The EU Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation (ESPR) mandates Digital Product Passports for products sold in Europe. Furniture is among the first categories, with deadlines approaching fast.

Product Connect is built from the ground up for this reality — not bolted onto legacy systems, but designed natively for the DPP framework, PEF methodology, and the way manufacturers actually work.

ESPR — Ecodesign for Sustainable Products

Framework regulation mandating DPPs across product categories, starting 2026.

PEF — Product Environmental Footprint

EU-aligned methodology for measuring and comparing environmental impact.

EPD — Environmental Product Declarations

Third-party verified impact reports. Product Connect generates EPD-ready exports.

CBAM — Carbon Border Adjustment

Cross-border carbon reporting for imported components and raw materials.
