Common questions

What exactly is a Hosted Product? +

A Hosted Product is any SKU you publish with a live Digital Product Passport on Product Connect. Each unique product (not variant or serial number) counts as one HP. If you sell 50 different chair models, that's 50 HPs — regardless of how many units you manufacture or how many times they're scanned.

Do scans really cost nothing? +

Yes. Every plan includes unlimited scans. When a consumer, retailer, or recycler scans your product's QR code, there's no per-view charge. We believe DPPs should reach everyone — charging per scan would create the wrong incentives.

How many tokens does a typical product need? +

A simple dining chair (15 components) uses ~15 tokens. A complex sofa system with 80+ components uses ~80 tokens. Tokens are consumed once per impact calculation — you only recalculate when a component changes (e.g., new supplier or material).

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime? +

Absolutely. Upgrade instantly — your new plan takes effect immediately and we prorate the difference. Downgrading takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. Your data and DPPs are preserved regardless.

Is this ESPR / EU DPP compliant? +

Yes. Product Connect is built from the ground up for ESPR compliance. Our DPP structure follows the EU's Digital Product Passport framework, including PEF-aligned environmental impact data, repairability information, and material provenance — all the way to EPD-ready exports on Pro and Enterprise plans.

What happens to my DPPs if I cancel? +

Your DPPs remain live for 30 days after cancellation. You can export all your data at any time. We'll send reminders before anything goes offline, and we'll work with you to ensure a smooth transition.
